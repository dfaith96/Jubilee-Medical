The platform

One connected workspace for everyday healthcare.

Patients use Jubilee Medical to find trusted care, keep their medical history, and follow up after visits. Providers use the same system to manage appointments, EHR, e-prescriptions, billing, referrals, reports, and patient communication.

  • Electronic health records that follow the patient
  • Appointment booking, teleconsultation, and visit notes
  • Pharmacy, laboratory, and payment workflows in one place

Core services

Simple features with complete healthcare coverage.

Appointments

EHR

Questions

Clear answers before launch.

Jubilee Medical is built for patients and healthcare providers who need a practical system that is easy to understand from day one.

Is Jubilee Medical for patients or providers?

Both. Patients use it to access care. Providers use it to manage appointments, records, billing, pharmacy, lab, and reporting.

Can hospitals use the EHR module?

Yes. The provider workspace supports patient files, consultation notes, prescriptions, lab requests, documents, and follow-up tasks.

Will the app support Nigerian payments?

Yes. The product is designed for local checkout, receipts, service payments, and provider billing workflows.

Is this a finished medical service?

This website presents the Jubilee Medical product concept and beta signup flow. Real clinical use should be reviewed by licensed medical and compliance teams before launch.
